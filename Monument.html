Berlin Monuments

Berlin is a city with a lot of history and past culture. That is why we are rich in buildings, monuments and recalls from our past

By: David Chaves Rodríguez :)

Update: 18/10/2022

Charlottenburg Palace

Placed in the outside of the city center. It was build around 1700. It is sorrounded by a beautiful garden and sits really close to the Charlottenburg museums.

Charlottenburg Palace, old residency of the Hohenzollern and biggest palace in Berlin

Now you can visit the dance room and the individual bedrooms in the Neuer Flügel, the fantastic collection of gold, silver and glass crockery that is shown in the Silberkammer and even some pieces of the prussian crown

Holocaust Memorial

Formed by 2711 concrete blocks, this monuments works as a memoriam for all the murdered jews during the horrific Holocaust

It is one of the most controversial monuments of the city as some people find it gloomy or dismal

I also contains information on the victims and locations, theme rooms as the Room of Dimensions, Room of families... and even historical footage that show the sites of persecution and extermination
